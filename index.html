The source data's "netQty100Days" field is actually a 90-day net sold quantity. Target 100d Stock = (Sold /90d ÷ 90) × 100 — the amount needed to cover 100 days at the current sell-through rate. Excess = current stock beyond that target, valued at unit price, no pack rounding. Items with zero sales in the last 90 days are excluded here — they show up under Never Sold or Dead Stock instead.
